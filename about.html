About Mana Realm

Mana Realm is a franchise created by Marcelo Augusto Fries. It's a game and story based on a alternative universe where two deities battle in planet 'Mana' for their own goals to be set. The Wizards battle against the beings called Necronites, beings of pure malice that uses aethermancy to destroy and conquer.

Mana Realm : Prologue

Mana Realm: Prologue is a fast-paced magical shooter based on the universe of Mana Realm. Choose your Wizardry and fight for the cause! The game is still in development and will be probably released in 2026.

The Author

Marcelo Augusto Fries is a developer and game designer. Born in 1995 in a small town in Brazil, he relocated many times throughout the country during his lifetime, but some of his passions never changed: Art, books, movies, animated series, music, and games. As a young adult, he started his journey in programming. Then, envisioned all his passions could converge into one thing: Mana Realm.
