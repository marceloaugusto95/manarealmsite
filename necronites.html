Necronites

Sons of Ahriman, the Destroyer. Necronites are the main villains and enemies of planet Mana. Beings of pure destruction, born from their creator's scattered essence. They wield a corrupt form of magic that uses Aether as fuel. Their only purpose is to consume and destroy, to undo the creation of their eternal enemies, the Wizards. Physically taller and magically stronger than the Wizards, they are the instruments of a dark will, a relentless plague upon the Mana Realm.
