Wizards

Sons of Azura, the Creator. Wizards are the devoted students of the arcane, wielding mana with discipline and wisdom. They draw their power from the fundamental elements of creation—Fire, Water, Earth and Air. A Wizard's path is one of lifelong learning, seeking to understand the intricate details of their own power. They are the protectors of the realm and the beacons of hope in times of darkness.

Pyromancy

  • Basic Spell: Fire Ball

    • The classic fire ball cast.

  • Great Spell: Conflagration

    • Casts a barrage of flames, destroying everything in its path. Resembles the fire breath of a dragon.

Aquamancy

  • Basic Spell: Aqua Ball

    • Casts a small water ball.

  • Great Spell: Aqua Purge

    • Casts a great water purge, one of the deadliest spells ever made. The flow of water force the caster to automatically recast.

Geomancy

  • Basic Spell: Stone Throw

    • Cast a small stone to hit a target.

  • Great Spell: Earth Spike

    • Spikes appears from the ground in earthquake fashion. Covers a huge terrain.

Aeromancy

  • Basic Spell: Wind Cut

    • Cast a wind that slash the target it hits.

  • Great Spell: Whirlwind

    • Cast a strong whirlwind foward.
